Privacy Policy — Ministry Planner

Last updated: March 2026

1. Overview

Ministry Planner ("the App") is designed with your privacy in mind. The App does not collect, sell, or share your personal data with any third parties. Everything you record stays on your device and in your personal iCloud account. The developer has no servers and no access to your data.

2. Data You Enter

All data you enter in the App — including ministry hours, planned entries, Bible study notes, and your name — is stored locally on your device using Apple's standard storage mechanisms. This data never passes through any servers owned or operated by the developer.

3. iCloud Sync

If you are signed in to iCloud, the App uses iCloud Key-Value Storage to sync your data across your personal devices. This data is stored in your private Apple iCloud account and governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. The developer has no access to your iCloud data.

4. Biometric Authentication (Face ID / Touch ID)

The App offers an optional App Lock using Face ID or Touch ID. Biometric data is processed entirely by iOS and your device's secure enclave. The App never has access to your biometric data — it only receives a yes/no result from iOS.

5. Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, the App schedules local notifications on your device. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any external servers or services.

6. In-App Purchases

Optional tips ("Thank You" purchases) are processed entirely through Apple's App Store. The developer does not collect or store any payment information. Purchases are governed by Apple's Terms and Conditions.

7. Analytics & Tracking

Ministry Planner does not use any analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or tracking tools. We do not track you across apps or websites.

8. Data We Do Not Collect

  • Name, email, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or crash reports sent to third parties
  • Photos or contacts

9. Data Deletion

All data can be permanently deleted at any time via Settings → Reset All Data inside the App. To also remove synced iCloud data, delete the App from all your devices.

10. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

11. Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the new version will be published here and noted in the App Store listing. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

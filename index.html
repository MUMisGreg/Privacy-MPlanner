Privacy Policy

Ministry Planner

Last updated: March 2026
The short version Ministry Planner does not collect your personal data. Everything you record stays on your device and in your personal iCloud account. The developer has no servers and no access to your data.

📝 Data You Enter

All data you enter in the app — including ministry hours, planned entries, Bible study notes, and your name — is stored:

  • Locally on your device using standard iOS storage
  • In your personal iCloud account (if signed in with an Apple ID), for the purpose of syncing across your own devices

This data never passes through any servers owned or operated by the developer.

☁️ iCloud Sync

If you are signed in to iCloud, the app uses iCloud Key-Value Storage to sync your data across your personal devices. This data is stored in your private Apple iCloud account. The developer has no access to your iCloud data.

Apple's Privacy Policy: apple.com/legal/privacy

🔒 Biometric Authentication

The app offers an optional App Lock using Face ID or Touch ID. Biometric data is processed entirely by iOS and your device's secure enclave. The app never has access to your biometric data — it only receives a yes/no result from iOS.

🔔 Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, the app schedules local notifications on your device. These are generated locally and do not involve any external servers or services.

💜 In-App Purchases

Optional tips ("Thank You" purchases) are processed entirely through Apple's App Store. The developer does not collect or store any payment information.

Apple's Terms: apple.com/legal/internet-services/itunes

🛡️ What We Do Not Collect

  • No analytics or crash reporting
  • No advertising SDKs
  • No account registration required
  • No location data
  • No access to contacts or photos
  • No data is sent to any server — the developer has no servers

🗑️ Data Deletion

All data can be permanently deleted at any time via Settings → Reset All Data inside the app. To also remove synced iCloud data, delete the app from all your devices.

👶 Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

📄 Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the new version will be published here and noted in the App Store listing. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
